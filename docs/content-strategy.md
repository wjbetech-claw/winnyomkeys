# Content strategy

## Brand / voice guidelines

The brand should feel:

- calm
- precise
- technically competent
- premium without sounding luxurious for its own sake
- honest rather than sales-heavy

## Messaging rules

Do:

- explain what is offered in plain language
- state Seoul, Korea clearly
- guide visitors toward the inquiry path
- set expectations without pretending certainty where there is none

Do not:

- invent testimonials
- invent certifications
- invent turnaround times
- invent pricing
- use hype-heavy startup language

## Landing page section plan

1. **Hero**
   - what WinnyomKeys does
   - where it operates
   - direct contact CTA
2. **Value proposition**
   - why the service is credible and useful
3. **Services**
   - repairs, builds, tuning, troubleshooting, guidance
4. **Audience fit**
   - who the service is for
5. **Workflow**
   - simple explanation of inquiry-to-completion path
6. **FAQ**
   - practical concerns and scope clarity
7. **Contact CTA**
   - what information a customer should send first
8. **Footer**
   - concise business positioning
