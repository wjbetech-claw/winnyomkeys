# Project overview

## Product

WinnyomKeys is a landing page for a Seoul-based personal business focused on mechanical keyboard repair, build, modding, tuning, and troubleshooting.

## Business goals

1. Explain the service clearly in a few seconds.
2. Build trust through calm, professional presentation.
3. Make contact easy.
4. Create a codebase that is easy to extend later.

## Target audience

- Keyboard owners with malfunctioning or inconsistent boards
- Enthusiasts planning custom builds
- Customers who want tuning or mod work
- Beginners who need guidance before buying or upgrading
- Visitors in or around Seoul, Korea who want clear communication

## V1 feature scope

- Single-page landing page
- Clear hero/value proposition
- Services overview
- Audience fit / reasons to choose the service
- Simple workflow explanation
- FAQ section
- Contact CTA
- Basic SEO metadata and crawlability support

## Out of scope for V1

- Online checkout or payment flow
- Booking engine
- User accounts
- Inventory system
- CMS integration
- Analytics beyond future follow-up work
- Fabricated testimonials, pricing, or turnaround claims

## Visitor journey

1. Visitor lands on the site.
2. Immediately sees what WinnyomKeys is and where it is based.
3. Scans whether their issue or project fits the service.
4. Understands how the process works.
5. Gains confidence from clear, honest copy.
6. Uses the inquiry CTA.

## Definition of done for V1 launch

- Messaging is honest and clear.
- Contact path is real and functional.
- Layout works cleanly on mobile and desktop.
- The page passes lint, typecheck, and build validation.
- Documentation is enough for a new contributor to continue safely.
- Placeholder business details are either replaced or clearly marked.
